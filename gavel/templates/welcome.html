Welcome to Gavel.

Gavel is a judging platform that makes expo judging easier. Judges are asked to compare each project they review against the previous project and make a simple decision on which of the two is better. By looking at your comparison patterns as well as those of the other judges, Gavel determines rankings for all of the participating projects.

Gavel does not take into consideration any specific judging criteria set by event staff or the possible weighting of such criteria in the evaluation of projects.
